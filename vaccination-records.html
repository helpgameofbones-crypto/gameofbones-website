📌 VACCINATION SCHEDULE (Puppies):
• 6-8 weeks: First DHPP/9-in-1 dose (₹400-700)
• 10-12 weeks: Second dose
• 14-16 weeks: Third dose + Rabies (mandatory by law)
• Annual: Booster shots every year

⚠️ RABIES IS MANDATORY: All dogs must be vaccinated against rabies by 12-16 weeks. This is required by Indian law.
