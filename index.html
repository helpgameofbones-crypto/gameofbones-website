Transparency

Our Process

Every step visible. No secrets. No shortcuts.

01
Sourcing
Human-Grade Ingredients Only
We source exclusively from FSSAI-certified suppliers. Every ingredient meets human consumption standards — the same quality you’d find at your butcher or fish market. Single origin, traceable, fresh. We visit suppliers personally and will not compromise on source quality regardless of cost.
🍎
02
Preparation
Hand-Prepared, Zero Chemicals
Every piece is cleaned and prepared by hand. No bleach wash. No chemical treatment. No preservative spray. We clean with plain water and prepare each batch fresh on the day it goes into the dehydrator. For fish, we debone where required. For organs, we trim excess fat for ideal texture after dehydration.
✏️
03
Dehydration
Slow-Dried at 55–70°C
This is where we differ from most commercial treats. Our dehydration runs at 55–70°C for 12–24 hours depending on the ingredient. This low, slow temperature removes moisture completely while preserving enzymes, amino acids, and micronutrients that high-heat baking destroys. The result is up to 95% nutrient retention versus around 50% for baked treats.
🌡️
04
Quality & Packing
Tested, Weighed, Sealed
Every batch is visually inspected before packing. We check for texture, colour consistency, and complete moisture removal. Treats are weighed accurately for each size. Packed in resealable, humidity-resistant zip-lock pouches designed for India’s monsoon climate. Each pouch carries a production date and best-before date.

What Can Be Returned / Refunded

Because our products are perishable food items, returns work differently from non-food products. Please read the following carefully.

✓ Eligible for Full Refund or Replacement
  • Wrong product delivered — you received a different product than what you ordered
  • Packaging damaged in transit — the pouch arrived torn, crushed or with the seal broken (photo required within 48 hours of delivery)
  • Product significantly different from description — e.g. contents do not match the labelled product at all
  • Missing items — one or more products from your order were not included in the shipment
  • Order not delivered — tracking shows delivered but you did not receive the parcel (must be reported within 7 days of the tracking delivery date)
✘ Not Eligible for Return or Refund
  • Change of mind after delivery — your dog did not like the treat, or you ordered by mistake
  • Opened or partially consumed products — for hygiene and food safety reasons, we cannot accept back any opened treat pouches
  • Incorrect storage after delivery — product quality issues resulting from improper storage (e.g. stored in a humid area without resealing)
  • Smell complaints without product defect — dehydrated meat and fish treats have a strong natural aroma. This is not a defect. The smell is a sign of quality.
  • Damage reported after 48 hours of delivery — transit damage must be reported with photos within 48 hours
  • COD orders refused at delivery — if a COD order is refused without a valid reason, we reserve the right to block future COD orders from the same address
