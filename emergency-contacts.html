💡 QUICK DECISION GUIDE
When to call which number:
🚨 Bleeding/unconscious/not breathing → Call 112 FIRST
🤕 Injured but alert → Call rescue (Karuna, SOS, ASHA)
🦷 Bite/fight injury → SOS, Karuna, ASHA
🪱 Sick/weak but stable → Visit veterinary clinic
🦅 Bird/wildlife/snake → Call specialized org

Prevention is Key: Clean feeding areas • Sterilize animals • Vaccinate against rabies • Report cruelty
